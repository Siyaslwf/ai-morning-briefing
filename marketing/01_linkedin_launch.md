## Engagement playbook (CRITICAL)

The first 60 minutes after posting decide whether LinkedIn shows it to
1,000 people or 100,000. So:

1. **Post the comment with the link IMMEDIATELY** (within 30 seconds)
2. **Reply to EVERY comment in the first hour** — 1-line replies are fine, just engage
3. **Like every comment** — even the simple "great work!" ones
4. **DM 10 friends/contacts** asking them to comment ANYTHING (not just like) — comments boost reach 5x more than likes
5. **Don't edit the post** for the first hour — edits reset the algorithm boost
6. **Re-share to your story** after 1 hour
