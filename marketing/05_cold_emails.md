# Cold Email Templates — Consulting Outreach

**Goal:** Land 1 paying client (€500–€1,500) within 7 days.

**Targets:** Latvian/Baltic small businesses where a daily AI briefing has obvious value.
See `06_target_list_template.md` for how to build the prospect list.

**Send rate:** 10 emails/day max. More = spam folder.

**Send from:** Your personal Gmail (`siyasmohammedd@gmail.com`) — better deliverability than a new domain.

**Follow-up rule:** One follow-up after 3 days. Never more.

## When you get on a call

1. **Spend the first 5 minutes listening** to their current pain
2. Don't pitch the product — pitch the outcome (saves them time, surfaces
   opportunities they're missing)
3. Show them the GitHub repo as proof it works
4. Quote €500 with confidence. If they push back, offer €350 for a
   "starter version" but never below that
5. Ask for payment upfront (50%) — separates real clients from tire-kickers
6. Always end with: "Who else in your network might benefit from something
   like this?"

---

## Realistic expectations

- 10 emails → ~2-3 replies → 1 call → 50% close rate
- So 10-20 emails per paid client. Expect to send 30 to land your first one.
- First client is the hardest. After you can say "I built this for X
  company, here's their testimonial" — conversion doubles.
