FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this actually free?

Yes. The free tier is funded by the open-source community and a few generous Pro subscribers. No card required.

Who writes it?

An AI pipeline I built — Gemini for research and writing, Pollinations.ai for the illustrations, Gmail SMTP for delivery. The whole system is open source on GitHub.

Can I run my own version?

Absolutely. The entire codebase is MIT-licensed on GitHub. Fork it, customize the topics, run it on your own infrastructure for free.

What if I don't like it?

One-click unsubscribe at the bottom of every email. No questions, no follow-up.

I want a custom version for my company.

I build custom AI newsletter systems (daily competitor monitoring, industry pulse, market intel) for companies. Reply to any newsletter or email hello@yourdomain.com.
